Key Technical Projects

2026

High-Density Pure Al MIM (99.5%)

Developed additive-free 99.5% pure aluminum MIM process achieving ≥97% relative density at 20–160 kg industrial batch scale. Expected thermal conductivity approaching 228 W/m·K — ideal for EV/ADAS thermal management.

  • • Kinetic-mechanical oxide disruption via super-solidus liquid phase sintering (SLPS)
  • • Critical particle size: D50 ≥ 11 μm
  • • Excellent post-processing (anodizing, laser/rod welding)
2026

High-Density Ti-Fe MIM using TiH₂-Fe

Cost-effective Ti MIM process achieving ≥97% relative density at 24 kg batch scale without HIP or external hydrogen.

  • • In-situ dehydrogenation of TiH₂ + transient liquid phase sintering with Fe
  • • α+β microstructure with suppressed grain boundary cracking
  • • Significant cost reduction vs. traditional gas-atomized spherical Ti powder
